TERMS OF SERVICE

1. About ShopGhana

ShopGhana is a Ghanaian e-commerce platform that enables local businesses to create online stores within minutes. The service is free with no account fees for basic store creation and operation.

2. Prohibited Items (Strictly Enforced)

To maintain a safe environment for all users, the following items are strictly prohibited for sale on ShopGhana:

  • Illegal drugs, narcotics, or prescription-only medications.
  • Weapons, firearms, ammunition, or explosives.
  • Adult content, pornography, or sexually explicit services.
  • Counterfeit goods, replicas, or pirated software/media.
  • Stolen property or items obtained through fraudulent means.

Any store found listing these items will be permanently banned without prior notice.

3. Delivery & Logistics

ShopGhana does not manage delivery services. Store owners are responsible for verifying the legitimacy of delivery personnel. ShopGhana takes 0% commission from delivery fees; all delivery payments are between the store, the driver, and the customer.

4. Store URLs & App Scope

Store URLs (.me domains) are proprietary to ShopGhana and are accessible only within the mobile application. They are not indexed for public web browsers (Safari/Chrome).

5. Compliance & Local Laws

Users must comply with all Ghana Trade Commission regulations. By using this platform, you verify that your business operates within the legal framework of the Republic of Ghana.

PRIVACY POLICY

Information Collection

We collect your email, phone number, and store details. This data is used strictly to provide the marketplace service and facilitate customer contact.

Account & Data Deletion

We respect your right to be forgotten. You can delete your account at any time via the Settings screen in the mobile app.

  • Immediate Removal: Upon deletion, your store, product listings, and public URL are deactivated instantly.
  • Permanent Purge: All personal identifying information is purged from our active systems.

Third-Party Payments

Payments for premium features are handled exclusively via Apple/Google In-App Purchases. ShopGhana does not store your credit card or Mobile Money pin numbers on our servers.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.
